About Us

Thiagarajar School of Management (TSM)

Thiagarajar School of Management (TSM) is a premier business school located in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. With a rich legacy of academic excellence, TSM is committed to nurturing future leaders and fostering impactful research. It is known for its rigorous curriculum, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, providing a conducive environment for management education and research.

Doctoral Colloquium

The Doctoral Colloquium '25 is an annual event organized by TSM, providing a unique opportunity for doctoral students and early-career researchers to present their ongoing work, receive constructive feedback from distinguished scholars and editors, and network with peers. It aims to foster a supportive environment for intellectual exchange and research development.

Submission

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit their original research papers and extended abstracts. Submissions should adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Papers must be in English.
  • Maximum word count: 8,000 words for full papers, 2,500 words for extended abstracts.
  • Formatting: APA style, 12-point Times New Roman, double-spaced.
  • All submissions will undergo a double-blind peer review process.

Venue & Accommodation

Madurai

Madurai, often referred to as the "Athens of the East," is one of India's oldest continuously inhabited cities. Steeped in history and rich cultural heritage, it is famous for the majestic Meenakshi Amman Temple, a stunning example of Dravidian architecture. The city's vibrant markets, traditional cuisine, and historical significance make it a captivating destination.
